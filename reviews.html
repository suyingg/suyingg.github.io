We designed our framework after intensive consultation and collaboration with many teachers and parents. The maximum score for each category is 5 and the minimum is 1.

A score that is 16 and above means that the book is highly recommended. If the score is 12-16, the book will only be recommended as “extra” assessment. If the book attracts a score of 12 and below, exercising our stringent standards, we will not recommend the book at all.
